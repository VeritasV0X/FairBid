# FairBid AI Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: June 30, 2026

FairBid AI helps homeowners understand home repair issues, compare fair local pricing, learn the normal repair process, identify safe DIY options, and know when to call a professional. This draft should be reviewed by a qualified attorney before publication.

## Information We Collect

FairBid AI may collect the following information when you use the app:

- Issue descriptions you type into the app.
- Photos you choose to upload for homeowner guidance and fair-price estimation.
- Approximate location information, such as city, state, ZIP code, latitude, and longitude, to estimate local pricing.
- In-app purchase status, such as whether you purchased a homeowner-check credit.
- Basic technical information needed to operate the app and backend.

## How We Use Information

We use this information to:

- Generate fair local price ranges and homeowner repair guidance.
- Analyze likely repair scope, cost drivers, and safety concerns from photos and descriptions.
- Adjust estimated pricing for local market conditions.
- Process in-app homeowner-check credits.
- Improve reliability, security, and app functionality.

## AI Processing

If AI homeowner-check generation is enabled, your issue description, selected photos, project category, and location may be sent to an AI service provider to generate guidance. Do not upload photos that contain sensitive personal information you do not want processed.

## Location

FairBid AI requests location access only while you are using the app. Location is used to compare local labor, materials, travel, and demand-related pricing.

## Photos

FairBid AI only accesses photos you choose through the photo picker. The app does not scan your full photo library.

## Purchases

Homeowner checks are sold as consumable in-app purchases. Purchase processing is handled by Apple.

## Data Retention

The starter version of FairBid AI does not require long-term storage of photos. A production backend should delete uploaded photos after estimate generation unless the user explicitly chooses to save report history.

## No Professional Guarantee

FairBid AI provides informational homeowner guidance and fair-price estimates only. It is not a licensed contractor, inspector, engineer, electrician, plumber, HVAC technician, safety certification, or binding quote provider. Actual bids may vary. Dangerous, code-regulated, or unclear issues should be handled by a qualified professional.
